At Optimizely, we're always looking for ways to make experimentation more seamless, intuitive, and integrated into your daily workflow. When we introduced the Optimizely MCP Server, we brought experimentation into the developer's Integrated Development Environment (IDE). Today, we're opening it up to the rest of your team with the Optimizely Remote MCP Server for Experimentation. It's a shift in how your whole team interacts with experimentation. Imagine managing feature flags, querying experiment results, and reporting on your program's health, directly from the AI tools you already use.
