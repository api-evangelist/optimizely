Three experiments that show how presenting options, surfacing prices, and using video can change which offer visitors choose and whether they choose at all. WHO: Digital, growth & CRO teams INDUSTRY: Business & Financial Services IMPACTED PAGES: Offer page, quote page Experiment 1: Repositioning tiers on the quote page Hypothesis: If we reposition or highlight a tier (for example, platinum tier) on the quote page, then more users will select Platinum cover because visual prominence and nudges increase consideration of premium options.
