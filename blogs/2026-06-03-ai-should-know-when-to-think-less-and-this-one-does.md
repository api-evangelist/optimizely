Optimizely developed an intelligent routing system called "Auto mode" that automatically determines whether prompts need full context enrichment or can be answered efficiently with lighter models, reducing both latency and token costs without sacrificing quality for simple queries. The approach matches computational effort to task complexity, demonstrating that a fast, economical answer is often the better answer.
