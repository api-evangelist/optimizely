WHO: E-commerce & CRO teams INDUSTRY: Retail IMPACTED PAGES: Search results page, Product Listing Pages Experiment 1: Redirecting zero-result searches to relevant recommendations Hypothesis: When customers see no search results, they often leave or disengage.
